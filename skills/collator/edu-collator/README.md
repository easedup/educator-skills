## 2. Setting up your AI Assistant

To use these skills, you need to upload the individual `.zip` files to your preferred AI assistant.

### Step 1: Unzip your download (done)
You've already unzipped the download (becuase you are reading this). So, you also have all the individual `.zip` files for each skill.

### Step 2: Upload to your AI
Upload the chosen `.zip` files directly to your preferred assistant:

#### For Claude (Skills work on free plan)
1. Log in to [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) or open your desktop app.
2. Go to [Claude Skills](https://claude.ai/customize/skills) (found under `Customize` in the sidebar menu).
3. Select the `'+'` button and select `'Upload a skill'`.
4. Upload the `easedup-edu-collator.zip` first, followed by any other skills you wish to use.

#### For ChatGPT (Skills only work on paid plan)
1. Log in to [ChatGPT.com](https://chatgpt.com) or open your desktop app.
2. Go to [ChatGPT Skills](https://chatgpt.com/skills).
3. Select the `'+'` button and select `'Upload from your computer'`.
4. Upload the `easedup-edu-collator.zip` first, followed by any other skills you wish to use.

### Step 3: Verify installation
Your skills will appear in your skills list and can be toggled on or off. Custom skills you upload are private to your individual account.
