# Inquiry

Skills for designing inquiry-based and project-based learning units.

- **[pbl-unit](./pbl-unit/SKILL.md)** — Creates a complete project-based learning (PBL) unit structured around a driving question, real-world context, inquiry phases, milestones, and a public product. Use when the unit centres on a student-created product or real-world audience.
- **[ibl-unit](./ibl-unit/SKILL.md)** — Creates a complete inquiry-based learning (IBL) unit structured around a central inquiry question, an inquiry cycle, and student-generated understanding. Supports structured, guided, and open inquiry models.
